Website Review Page

Welcome to the gift ideas generator!


Our mission is to relieve the stress of having to come up with gift ideas for any occasion. Stuck for present ideas for somebody who seems to have everything? or need last minute gift ideas? then you're definitely come to the right place. Our suggestion lists contain gifts that are perfect as birthday present ideas, anniversary present ideas, retirement gifts or any other occasion you care to imagine.


We do not have our own products to promote, so we are able to suggest any gift under the sun. We have ideas ranging from ‘Mow their lawn Voucher’, to top end ideas such as ‘A trip on the orient express’ and ethical ideas like ‘sponsor a goat for a 3rd world family’.


If you're looking for christmas gift suggestions, then feel free to check out our christmas gift guide. Our gift ideas generator is very easy to use - just use the above controls to indicate how much you want to spend on the gift, the age of the recipient, their character type (this column is optional, and you can click on up to three characters), and finally the gender of the recipient, be it a gift for him, gift for her, gifts suitable for either gender, or gifts for couples. Then just press 'Search' to get loads of suitable suggestions!


If you see something on the suggested list that you like, just click on it to be taken to the relevant page on our top recommended website for that gift idea. You can also hover over the 'websites', 'price comparison' or 'shops' columns to see a list of links to recommended websites, price comparison sites and high street shops. The website links will take you directly to the correct page on the suggested sites for the present ideas, the price comparison links will search the sites for the relevant gifts, and the high street shop links will take you to the 'store finder' page of the shop's website, or to a local directory site which will search for the store.


The list by can be filtered by category, by changing the drop down selection on the top left of the screen. The 'Next', 'Prev', 'First' and 'Home' buttons down in the bottom right of the screen to go back and forth through the gift ideas list, and to come back to the home page.


Alternatively, you may prefer to use our gift ideas directory instead.
